Spicy Garlic Noodles

more_horiz

Recipe

timer

10 min

Prep

soup_kitchen

15 min

Cook

groups

2-3

Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 bundles instant ramen noodles (discard seasoning packets)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp chili flakes (or to taste)
  • 1/4 cup reserved noodle water
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

1

Cook ramen noodles according to package directions. Drain, reserving 1/4 cup of the noodle water. Set noodles aside.

2

In a large skillet or wok, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

3

Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, and chili flakes. Mix well until sugar dissolves.

4

Add the cooked noodles and reserved noodle water to the skillet. Toss everything together until the noodles are evenly coated with the sauce.

5

Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds before serving hot.
