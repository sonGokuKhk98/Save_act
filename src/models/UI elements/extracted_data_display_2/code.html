Rome City Break

Travel

Duration

3 Days / 2 Nights

Destination

Rome, Italy

Start Date

Oct 26, 2024

Travelers

2 Adults

Day 1: Ancient Wonders

expand_more

Morning:

  • Visit the Colosseum & Roman Forum (Book tickets in advance!)
  • Explore Palatine Hill for historical views.

Afternoon:

  • Walk to the Pantheon and marvel at its architecture.
  • Grab a gelato near Piazza Navona.

Evening:

  • Dinner in Trastevere: Try 'Cacio e Pepe' at a local trattoria.
  • Evening stroll by the Tiber River.

Day 2: Vatican City & Baroque Beauty

expand_more

Morning:

  • Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel (Pre-book skip-the-line tickets).
  • St. Peter's Basilica: Climb to the dome for city views.

Afternoon:

  • Throw a coin in the Trevi Fountain.
  • Shop along Via del Corso.

Evening:

  • Pizza night near the Spanish Steps.
  • Enjoy an opera or classical concert.

Day 3: Hidden Gems & Departure

expand_more

Morning:

  • Explore the Borghese Gallery and Gardens (Reservation essential).
  • Rent a rowboat in the Borghese Lake.

Afternoon:

  • Last-minute souvenir shopping.
  • Enjoy a final Roman coffee.

Evening:

  • Depart from Rome.
